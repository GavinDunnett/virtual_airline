Origins & Purpose

Orginally create by a group of students at UT Dallas in 2024, Virtual Airline aims to address the need for an interactive and educational platform that simulates the complexities of airline management. Users face the challenge of maximizing profitability by making strategic decisions regarding aircraft selection, flight scheduling, and route management, all within the constraints of real-world economic variables and operational limitations. This game seeks to provide a realistic and engaging experience that enhances understanding of the airline industry's dynamics.
